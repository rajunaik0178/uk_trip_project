❓ Frequently Asked Questions

📞 Ask Us Something
Can't find your answer below? Send us a message and we'll respond within 24 hours.
📄 Bookings
How do I book a UK tour package? ▾
Browse packages from the Browse Packages page. Click Book Now on any package, select your preferred travel date, and confirm. Your booking will be sent for admin approval and you'll receive a notification once it's confirmed.
Can I cancel my booking? ▾
Yes — you can cancel a Pending (Booked) booking from My Bookings at any time before it is approved. Once approved, please contact us via Contact Us to request a cancellation.
How long does approval take? ▾
Bookings are typically reviewed within 24–48 hours. You will receive a notification in the app as soon as your booking is approved or updated.
Can I book multiple packages at the same time? ▾
Yes, you can book multiple different packages. However, you cannot have more than one pending booking for the same package at the same time.
💳 Payments
What payment methods are accepted? ▾
We accept Cash, UPI, Credit/Debit Card, and Bank Transfer. Payments are recorded by our admin team after your booking is approved.
How do I get a receipt or invoice? ▾
Once your booking is approved and payment is recorded, you can view your receipt from the My Bookings page by clicking the Receipt button. Admins can also print or email invoices from the admin panel.
Is my payment information secure? ▾
Yes. Payments are recorded manually by our admin team and no card details are stored on the platform. All data is handled securely.
🌍 Packages & Guides
What does a tour package include? ▾
Each package includes guided tours, accommodation arrangements, and transport. Full details are listed on each package card. For more specific information, use the Contact Us form.
What are Trip Guides? ▾
Trip Guides are detailed destination write-ups created by our team — covering itineraries, local tips, must-see places, and day-wise plans for popular UK destinations. View them from the Trip Guides page.
Will a personal tour guide be assigned to me? ▾
Yes — once your booking is approved, our admin will assign a certified tour guide to your trip. You will receive an in-app notification with the guide's name when the assignment is made.
Can I add a package to my wishlist? ▾
Yes! Click the ❤️ heart icon on any package card to add it to your Wishlist. Your wishlist is saved and accessible from the sidebar at any time.
👤 My Account
How do I update my profile? ▾
Go to My Profile from the sidebar. You can update your name, address, and mobile number. To change your password, enter your current password and then the new one.
How do I write a review? ▾
You can write a review only for bookings that have been Approved. Go to Write Review from the sidebar, select your booking, choose a star rating, and submit your feedback.
I forgot my password. What should I do? ▾
Please contact our support team via the Contact Us page and we will assist you with a password reset.
🆘 Support
How do I contact support? ▾
Use the Contact Us page to send a message directly to our team. We respond within 24 hours on working days. For urgent issues during your trip, call our helpline: +91 80000 00000.
What are your support hours? ▾
Our support team is available Monday to Saturday, 9am – 6pm IST. Emergency support is available 24×7 on +91 80000 00000.
